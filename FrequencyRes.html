Key Concepts

  • Frequency Resolution Δf = fₛ / N — increasing the number of observed samples sharpens the spectral peaks. Try dragging the Samples slider and watch the frequency plot.
  • Spectral Leakage — when the signal frequency is not an integer multiple of Δf, energy "leaks" into neighbouring bins. Set Signal 2 to 1.5 Hz to see this clearly.
  • Hamming Window tapers the edges of the signal to near-zero, greatly reducing leakage side-lobes at the cost of a slightly wider main peak.
  • Try this: Set Signal 2 → 1.5 Hz, Samples → 64, then toggle Rectangular ↔ Hamming and observe the leakage difference in the frequency plot.
